Songket

According to history, songket fabric originally emerged during the Sriwijaya Kingdom in Palembang from the 7th to the 13th century. The tradition of making songket fabric has continued to the present day. In addition to being sold in the form of fabric sheets, songket fabric is now also produced in ready-to-wear clothing models, shawls, and even hijabs.
